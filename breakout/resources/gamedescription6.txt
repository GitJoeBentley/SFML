Random Tiles

In this game, tile colors have different values and exhibit 
different behaviors when you hit one.

Red    (1 point)  Speeds up the ball by 10%
Orange (2 points) Slows the ball down by 10%
Yellow (3 points) Paddle size is increased by 10%
Green  (4 points) Paddle size is decreased by 10%
Blue   (5 points) Ball blanks out intermittently
Indigo (6 points) Ball jumps to the left or right
Violet (7 points) Ball bounce is unpredictable and ball speeds up

You get 10 balls and 109 seconds to go after the 109 tiles.

Good luck
